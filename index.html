ZeroLogs

It's none of our business

ZeroLogs is an independent non-profit which hosts a number of Tor nodes around the globe. Our mission is to increase the number of nodes on the Tor network.

We believe the Internet should be free from censorship and surveillance. As our names suggests, we don't keep logs and it's none of our business.

If you or your company is able to donate resources or funds to help us grow the network, please feel free to contact us!
